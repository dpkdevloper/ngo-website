Testimonials
Our supporting members and volunteers always makes us determined in our campaigns.

Mr. Brajesh jaiswal

C.E.O

Our partnership with JMDDCT started way back during the Tsunami Rehabilitation program days in 2006. REAL implemented a number of innovative livelihood initiatives such as the ‘goat bank’,social security initiatives enabling access to micro insurance and micro pension for the poor and marginalized households, the ‘Banking on Change’ program that provided access financial services through building the SHGs and their federations, and value chain development programs in cashew nut and dairy. During this period, REAL has also partnered with JMDDCT to respond to a number of natural calamities.

Miss Preeti

(Co-Founder)
Namrata universal

Namrata Universal which is a Corporate Social Responsibility wing of PI Industries Limited has been working with JMDDCT on Women Empowerment programme aimed at “ Entrepreneurship Development and Skill Enhancement” of underprivileged women of the self-help groups by access to finances, resources, opportunities and strengthening of traditional livelihood opportunities in U.P. . Many women and girls in the most backward regions of the country are not fortunate enough to leverage economic opportunities available to urban areas. JMDDCT continuously works for their empowerment and we are very proud of our association with them and the good work they do towards changing lives of many for better.
