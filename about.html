About us

Brief History of the Trust

Socio-economic empowerment of villages especially of weaker sections is the need of present day time. For such empowerment, it is the need of hour to start people's driven, area specific and need-based rural development pogrammes in the country and work without any regard to the race, religion, caste, creed or community.
121 crores people of India live in six lakh villages. It is well known that "the essential needs of villages today are water, power, road, sanitation, healthcare, education, transportation, communication and other services needed for sustainable development and quality of life." Besides, the struggle for essential needs are other issues related with empowerment of rural women, literacy,' education, communal harmony, etc.
Inadequate infrastructural facilities has always led to interstate migration which has further given rise to several other socio-economic & environmental problems due to excessive pressure on resources of other states. A need to address and to solve problems associated with aforesaid issues and to accelerate sustainable development of the country was felt by some intellectuals in the year 2019 whose outcome was the formation of jai maa durga dham charitable trust.
