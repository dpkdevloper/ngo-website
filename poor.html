HELP TO POORS CAMPAIGN BY JMDDCT

The sansthan has set up 01 educational center to educate Child labour and slums children of age group of 6 to 14 years. In this programme various educational aids like creative activities, playing, knowledge for social behavior and proper manners etc. In this center total 32 children have been enrolled so far and given various aids for their development. The above centre run at Raniganj of Pratapgarh district of Uttar Pradesh for Three month duration staring from 1st Nov 2019. The organization have run a 03 months Non Residential Bridge Course at Jamunipur village of Pratapgarh district in which 16 children of age group of 06 to 11 years. The organization have made arrangements for their Breakfast, Dress, Copy, Books, Pencil, School Bag and all other arrangement. The programme start from 10th Jan.,2020 and ended 09th March.2020.
