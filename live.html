Livelihood

Intervention by NMCT through its livelihood programmes in the last few decades has had a significant positive impact on the lives of the rural and tribal population. Focus on capacity building, fostering community links and promotion of small business are some few ways to build sustainable livelihood options for them. Inclusion of women in economic activities and encouraging women-owned entrepreneurial ventures in rural India has proved to be successful in eradicating issues related to livelihood of rural people. As farming and other agricultural activities still dominate the rural economy, several efforts are being taken by the NGOs to benefit from this economic activity. Initiatives focused on gender integration in agricultural practices, equipping the rural community with the latest technological trends in farming have helped them to resolve issues related to livelihood.

Women Entrepreneurship Development

Employing cutting-edge capabilities to positively impact marginalized lives by enhancing their financial and business literacy, life skills, employability, and entrepreneurship readiness in urban and semi-urban settings.

JMDDCT Women Entrepreneurship Development initiative aims to develop the capacity, capability, and occupational & entrepreneurial skillset of marginalised women to increase their readiness for starting their own ventures. Through partnerships and collaborations with various stakeholders including the government, NGOs, research institutions and corporate entities, we create an enabling environment for technological and institutional innovation, and to facilitate effective scaling up of interventions to achieve large scale impact.
