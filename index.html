Our Mission

To see peoples of uttar pradesh in rural and unpreviledged areas be healthy, literate, be-employed, self sufficient and self-reliance. Always help the needy.

ABOUT US

Brief History of the Trust its objectives Socio-economic empowerment of villages especially of weaker sections is the need of present day time. For such empowerment, it is the need of hour to start people's driven, area specific and need-based rural development pogrammes in the country and work without any regard to the race, religion, caste, creed or community. 131 crores people of India live in six lakh villages. It is well known that "the essential needs of villages today are water, power, road, sanitation, healthcare, education, transportation, communication and other services needed for sustainable development and quality of life." Besides, the struggle for essential needs are other issues related with empowerment of rural women, literacy,' education, communal harmony, etc. A need to address and to solve problems associated with aforesaid issues and to accelerate sustainable development of the country was felt by some intellectuals in the year 2019 whose outcome was the formation of JAY MAA DURGA DHAM CHARITABLE TRUST.

FIELDS OF CHANGE

EDUCATION

Education, nutrition, girl child education, right to education for every child and holistic development of children. Help orphan child to educate them.

HEALTH CARE

Taking healthcare services to doorsteps of hard to reach communities, Adolescent Sexual and Reproductive Health, PC & PNDT.

ENVIRONMENT

Plantation Drive- Go green Initiative, Heritage Conservation Drive, Clean Festival Drive, Development of Man Made forest, Save water.

LIVELIHOOD

Agriculture and rural livelihood transformation. Improving lifestyle of unpreviledged communities, for better lives.
